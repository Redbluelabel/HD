
DNA CpG methylation data can be obtained from the GEO database.

1)	Go to https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/geo/
2)	Select an appropriate dataset. For instance: GSE 147004
3)	Download the data 

The data needs to be in matrix/vector form with no missing values. 
The download from the GEO database will contain additional information that needs to be delete. 
A canonical indicator (vector) identifying every individual as a control (0) or patient (1) needs to be manually constructed.
